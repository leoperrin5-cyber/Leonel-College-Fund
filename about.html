About Me

My name is Leonel Perrin, and I will be attending Saint Leo University to study Biomedical and Health Sciences.

My dream is to become a pediatric doctor who provides the best care possible to children. I believe children are the future, and giving them the care, compassion, and medical support they deserve is one of the most meaningful ways to make a difference in the world.

I am committed to working hard, learning everything I can, and dedicating my life to helping young patients grow, heal, and thrive. Your support helps me begin this journey and focus on my education without financial stress.
